Name: Mindray
Classification: Device
Time to Research: 1:30
Prerequisites: Radio Transistor or Personal Cloak Generators
Leads to: Satchels, Tripmines

Another energy weapon developed by your scientists, this device works with highly experimental meta-neurotechnology. Depending on the desired effect, the beams emitted from the mindray can either disrupt or encourage the electrical impulses along neural pathways. The testing on this weapon has not been very thorough, as that it only seems to have effects on creatures with extremely high neural activity. When wielding this device be sure to have a steady hand and aim for the brain of the scientist so as to maximize the neurological effect.
