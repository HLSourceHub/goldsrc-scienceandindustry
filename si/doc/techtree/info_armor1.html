Name: Armor Upgrade Mk1
Classification: Armor Upgrade
Time to Research: 1:30
Prerequisites: Start Research
Leads to: Armor Upgrade Mk2, Regenerative Armor Tech
Gameplay: +25 to max armor, -2 to max speed

Armor is vital to the survival of your security guards, and will save you money on cloning expenses. This armor is the weakest grade, but it is still enough to give your employees a better chance when on duty.
