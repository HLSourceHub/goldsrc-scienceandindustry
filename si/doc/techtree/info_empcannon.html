Name: EMP Cannon
Classification: Device
Time to Research: 1:45
Prerequisites: Radio Transistor or Personal Cloak Generators
Leads to: Satchels, Tripmines

The EMP cannon comes with two fire modes: a discharge of energy and a grenade launcher mode. The basic discharge rapidly removes enemy armor, while the secondary launcher drains away armor as well as shortfusing nearby tripmines, satchels and radios.
