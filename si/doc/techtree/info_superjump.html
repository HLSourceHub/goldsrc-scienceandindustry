Name: Super Jump Module
Classification: Implant
Time to Research: 1:30
Prerequisites: Advanced Regeneration or Cybernetic Leg Implants Mk1 or Ammunition Replicators Mk1 or Bionic Muscle Fiber Replacements
Leads to: Nothing
Gameplay: This jump upgrade increases your jump velocity in the direction you face. Look up to perform a high jump, look in front of you to perform a long jump.

This module enables your employees to perform bigger jumps, allowing them to reach higher levels, jump over longer gaps, and move faster. This jump upgrade is the most effective combined with the Cybernetic Leg Implants (MkII). The super jump can be performed in the air if a wall is right behind you.
