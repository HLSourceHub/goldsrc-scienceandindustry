Name: Grenade
Classification: Weapon
Time to Research: 1:30
Prerequisites: Assault Shotgun or Tommygun or Akimbo snUZIs
Leads to: Crossbow, Tau Cannon, Rocket Pistol

This is your standard hand grenade. It has a three-second delay on the detonation, so be sure to throw it pretty quickly or it will blow up in your face.
