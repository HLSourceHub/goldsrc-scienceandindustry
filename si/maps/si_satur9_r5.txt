----Objectives----------------------

RECRUITMENT - There are 6 scientists in this map. Kidnap an enemy
scientist by hitting them with the briefcase. Take them back to the
administrator, and he/she will start working for your company instead.
(5 points)

ESPIONAGE - Steal the datastreamer from the doo-hickey by having a
teammate turn the thinga-majig and return to your administrator. You
will gain 2 1/2 minutes of research, and the rival team will lose
2 1/2 minutes of research. (3 points)
